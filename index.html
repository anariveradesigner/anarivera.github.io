About Me

Designing with
purpose & clarity

I'm a UX/UI designer who moved from education to designing clean and intuitive experiences. Honestly, the core skill is quite similar: understanding people needs and making the complex feel simple.

I approach every project with structured thinking, deep research, and more to-do-lists than I'd like to admit. When I'm not on Figma, I'm hunting for a good coffee spot or trying not to kill my plants.

My Approach

Design
Process

01

Discover

Deep research into users, context, and business goals. Interviews, surveys, and competitive analysis to uncover real problems.

02

Define

Synthesizing findings into clear problem statements, personas, and user journeys that guide every design decision.

03

Design

From rough sketches to high-fidelity prototypes — iterating quickly, testing early, and refining based on feedback.

04

Deliver

Polished handoff with design systems, specifications, and ongoing collaboration with developers to ensure quality execution.
